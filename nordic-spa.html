Nordic Spa & Sauna

Enjoy your own private Nordic spa experience nestled in the forest just steps from the Aux Box. The sauna, hot tub, cold plunge, and seasonal outdoor shower are for your use only during your stay.

Private use Sauna 70-90C Hydrate often
Important information
  • Use all spa amenities at your own risk.
  • The sauna, hot tub, and cold plunge expose your body to extreme temperatures and may not be suitable for everyone.
  • If you are pregnant or have medical concerns, please consult your physician before use.
  • By using these amenities, guests acknowledge and accept all associated risks.

Spa Etiquette

  • Please shower before using the sauna, hot tub, or cold plunge.
  • Rinse or wipe your feet before entering the hot tub or cold plunge.
  • Keep the covers on the hot tub and cold plunge when not in use.
  • No glassware in the spa area.
  • Stay hydrated and listen to your body.

Suggested Nordic Spa Circuit

  1. Relax in the sauna for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Cool down in the cold plunge or with a cold shower.
  3. Rest and rehydrate.
  4. Repeat as desired.

Hot and cold circuits are not suitable for everyone. Exit immediately if you feel dizzy, lightheaded, or unwell.

Sauna Instructions

The sauna control is located inside the Aux Box on the wall near the entrance door.

Turning the Sauna On

  1. Press the dial once to power on the sauna.
  2. Rotate the dial to select your desired temperature.
  3. Press the dial once to confirm and begin heating.

Recommended temperature: 70-90C

Timing

  • Allow approximately 20-30 minutes for the sauna to reach temperature.
  • For the best experience, turn the sauna on about 15-20 minutes before you plan to use it.

Sauna Lighting

To turn the sauna ambient light on or off, quickly double-press the dial.

Turning the Sauna Off

When finished, press the dial once to turn the sauna off.

Important sauna notes
  • Do not leave the sauna running when not in use.
  • Limit sauna sessions to 15-20 minutes at a time.
  • Stay hydrated before, during, and after use.
