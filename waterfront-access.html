Waterfront Access

Enjoy exclusive access to the Muskoka River during your stay. The waterfront area features a private dock, lounge chairs, and a canoe for exploring the river.

5-minute walk Private dock Canoe included

Before Leaving the Waterfront

Before heading back up the trail, take a moment to return the canoe gear and leave the dock area in the same condition you found it.

Important information and safety
  • Use the waterfront, dock, and canoe at your own risk.
  • River conditions can vary throughout the year.
  • While the Muskoka River is typically calm, water levels and current may be stronger during certain seasons or following significant rainfall.
  • If you are inexperienced with canoeing, we recommend paddling upstream from the dock first so the return trip is easier with the current.
  • The owners of the Aux Box assume no liability for injuries, accidents, or losses resulting from use of the waterfront, dock, canoe, or surrounding area.
