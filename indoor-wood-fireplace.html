Indoor Wood Fireplace

Indoor wood fireplaces can be challenging to start, especially if they are unfamiliar. Review the video and use the tips below before lighting your first fire.

Tips for a Successful Fire

  • Open the patio door slightly while getting the fire started to help establish a strong draft.
  • Start with very small pieces of kindling and wood.
  • Gradually add larger pieces once the fire is burning well.
  • Avoid placing large logs into the fireplace when starting a fire.

Troubleshooting

If outdoor temperatures are mild, it may be more difficult to establish a draft and get the fire going.
