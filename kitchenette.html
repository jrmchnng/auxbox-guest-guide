Drinking Water

The tap water is fresh Muskoka well water and is safe to drink.

Dish Soap

Dish soap is available in the built-in soap dispenser located to the left of the faucet.

Coffee

Enjoy a fresh cup of coffee during your stay with the provided Hatch Coffee Roasters coffee, kettle, and French press.

French press instructions

Important

Please do not place the French press on the induction cooktop or BBQ. It is not designed to be heated.
