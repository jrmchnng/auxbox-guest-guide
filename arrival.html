Arrival

Use these steps to get parked, find the trail, and access the Aux Box smoothly.

Important

Please ensure your GPS routes you to Markles Road via Brunel Road.

Arrival Guide

Address: 454 Markles Road, Huntsville, ON

Park beside the Aux Box sign and follow the trail to the cabin. It is a short walk from the designated parking area to the Aux Box.

Quick Steps

Step 1 Park beside the concrete Aux Box sign in the designated parking area.
Step 2 Follow the trail beside the sign through the forest to the cabin.
Step 3 Open your booking app or portal to find the access code before you arrive.

Access Code

  • Airbnb guests: find your code in the Airbnb app under Check-In Information.
  • Direct bookings and other platforms: your code can be found in your guest portal.
