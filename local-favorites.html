Our Local Favorites

These recommendations are in or around Huntsville and are approximately 10 minutes from the Aux Box.

Dining Hiking Winter activities
Restaurants
  • Tall Trees

    One of Huntsville's most popular upscale dining experiences. Do not miss the private winter dining huts with wood-burning fireplaces.

  • Allora

    A stylish and lively restaurant known for wood-fired pizza, fresh pasta, and creative cocktails.

  • Mottboro Sushi

    Our go-to recommendation for sushi and a great takeout option.

  • Fox and the Hen House

    A local food truck serving creative comfort food and crowd favorites.

  • Smokin' Hot BBQ

    A unique Muskoka dining experience serving delicious slow-smoked meats.

  • Little Place by the Lights

    A local favorite for pizza and pasta.

  • Belly Ice Cream

    Known for creative small-batch ice cream flavors that change weekly.

Favorite activities around Huntsville
  • Lions Lookout

    One of Huntsville's most iconic viewpoints with panoramic views of the area.

  • Limberlost Forest & Wildlife Reserve

    Beautiful forest trails, lakes, wildlife, and hiking for all skill levels.

  • Arrowhead Provincial Park

    Scenic hiking trails, swimming, canoeing, and wildlife viewing.

  • Salt Yoga

    A welcoming boutique yoga studio in downtown Huntsville.

  • Downtown Huntsville

    Explore local boutiques, cafes, waterfront restaurants, and Muskoka charm.

Winter favorites
  • Hidden Valley Highlands Ski Area

    Downhill skiing and snowboarding just minutes from the Aux Box.

  • Arrowhead Provincial Park

    Home to outdoor skating trails, snowshoeing, cross-country skiing, and winter hiking.

  • North Ridge Ranch

    Experience dog sledding through the Muskoka wilderness.
